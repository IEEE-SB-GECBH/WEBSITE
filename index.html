About Our College

GEC BARTON HILL

Government Engineering College, Barton Hill was established by the Government of Kerala in the academic year 1999-2000. Within a short span of time since inception, the college has achieved notable academic excellence. The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University for all  batches. It is under the administrative control of the Director of Technical Education, Government of Kerala. The present Principal of the college is Dr. Bijulal D.

Visit our college website for more details:- www.gecbh.ac.in
