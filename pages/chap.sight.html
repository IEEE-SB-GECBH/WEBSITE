ABOUT THE CHAPTER




IEEE SIGHT (Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology) stands as a global network of dedicated IEEE volunteers, united in their mission to confront diverse humanitarian challenges. They leverage technology to enhance the well-being of underserved communities globally. Within this context, the Student Branch has consistently exhibited a fervent dedication to orchestrating humanitarian endeavours, ultimately leading to the establishment of the SIGHT Chapter within the Student Branch in 2022.

Since its inception, the SIGHT Chapter of the Student Branch has operated as a dedicated cadre of volunteers whose efforts have been directed towards a multitude of projects and initiatives, all aimed at elevating the conditions of underserved communities. This includes the noble endeavour of disseminating education in rural areas through engaging STEM activities and facilitating access to modern, digitised methods of learning.Through a wealth of meticulously organised events and activities, the Chapter not only encourages its members to cultivate innovative ideas that can drive positive societal change but also instill a profound sense of social commitment among emerging professionals, thus making a meaningful contribution to the betterment of humanity.
